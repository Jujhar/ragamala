Retro: Tukhari (3), Ambika / Madhuvanti (3).

  • (5) Sri raga ⛵💚(3 ☿🌜)

Wives: Baradi / Varali / Bairari (2 ♀️🪙), Karnati (0 DR), Gavri (2 SP), Asavari (1 🪙♀️), Sindhve / Sindhura (3 SP).

  • (6) Megh ☔(L 🔅🌜)

Wives: Sorath (R1 ☿🪙), Gond (1), Malari (L ☿🔅?), Asa (R3 ♂🔅), Soohou (Puniya? or Soob).

Retro: Vibhas (0).
Combo: Asa Aaswari (Sri) (1?), Tribungi, Sorath, Gujri (Deepak), Shalokh, Nat Bhariav.
